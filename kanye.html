Kanye West

My Story

I am Warhol! I am the #1 most impactful artist of our generation! I am Shakespeare in the flesh, WALT DISNEY! Nike! GOOGLE!! I am from Chicago, Illinois and am 40 years young. I have a wife (Kim Kardashian-West), and 2 kids (North and Saint). My fame started as a producer for Roc-A-Fella records, and evolved as I became a Grammy-award winning musician with 8 legendary albums. I am a fashion designer, most known for my Yeezy season collection - including the rare and sought-after Yeezy sneakers.

Hobbies

I like rapping, producing, and fashion. I began as one of the greatest producers in history, and people tried to marginalize me when I started trying to get into rap. So I became one of the greatest rappers in history. People are now trying to marginalize me as I become one of the greatest fashion icons in history.

My Favorite Memory
